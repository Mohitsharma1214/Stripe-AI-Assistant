Refunds
Refund objects allow you to refund a charge that has previously been created but not yet refunded.

The Refund Object Attributes:
- id (string): Unique identifier for the object.
- amount (integer): Amount, in cents.
- balance_transaction (nullable string): ID of the balance transaction that describes the impact of this refund on your account balance.
- charge (nullable string): ID of the charge that's being refunded.
- currency (string): Three-letter ISO currency code.
- reason (enum): Reason for the refund. One of duplicate, fraudulent, or requested_by_customer.
- status (string): Status of the refund. For example, succeeded, pending, failed, or canceled.

Main Endpoints:
- POST /v1/refunds: Create a refund.
    Parameters: charge, amount, payment_intent, reason, metadata.
- GET /v1/refunds/:id: Retrieve a refund.
- GET /v1/refunds: List all refunds.
